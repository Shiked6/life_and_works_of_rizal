Chapter 1

Advent of a National Hero

Dr. Jose Rizal is a unique example of a many-splendored genius who became the greatest hero of a nation. enddowed by god with versatile gifts, he truly ranked with the world's geniuses.

Dr. Jose Rizal's Characteristics

  1. Physician (Ophthalmic Surgeon)
  2. Poet
  3. Dramatist
  4. Essayist
  5. Novelist
  6. Historian
  7. Architect
  8. Painter
  9. Sculptor
  10. Educator
  11. Linguist
  12. Musician
  13. Naturalist
  14. Ethnologist
  15. Surveyor
  16. Engineer
  17. Farmer Businessman
  18. Economist
  19. Geographer
  20. Cartographer
  21. Bibliophile
  22. Philologist
  23. Grammarian
  24. Folklorist
  25. Philosopher
  26. Translator
  27. Inventor
  28. Magician
  29. Humorist
  30. Satirist
  31. Polemicist
  32. Sportsman
  33. Traveler
  34. Prophet

Above and beyond all these, he was a hero and political martyr who consecrated his life for the redemption of his oppressed people. No wonder, he is now acclaimed as the national hero of the Philippines.

The Birth of a Hero

Jose Rizal was born on the moonlit night of Wednesday, June 19, 1861, in the lakeshore town of Calamba, Laguna Province, Philippines.
